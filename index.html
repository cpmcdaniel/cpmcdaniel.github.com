This site is dedicated to the thoughts and opinions of yours truly, Craig McDaniel - a former polyglot software engineer on a long journey to find the best way to write software systems. Throughout my career, I have often found myself asking questions like, "Is this the best we [as an industry] can do? Is this the best way to write good quality software?"

My path toward enlightenment has led me to functional programming - specifically Clojure. In these pages I will attempt to explain the rationale behind my love affair with this beautiful and powerful language, and the ecosystem of projects and libraries surrounding it. Whether you agree or disagree, I hope my musings will inspire you to think about things in a different light, and perhaps question things you simply assumed were "best practice" facts about developing good software.
